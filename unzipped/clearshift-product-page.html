The product

What is smart film?

Switchable film — known technically as PDLC (Polymer-Dispersed Liquid Crystal) — is a self-adhesive film that bonds to the interior side of existing glass. Apply 60V AC and the crystals align, making it crystal clear. Cut the power and they scatter, frosting the glass for total privacy.

It retrofits to almost any existing window, partition or door without replacing the glass. Switches in under a second. Operates from a switch, remote, or smartphone app. Lasts 7–10 years properly installed.

Installation

Your installer fits it. Your sparky wires it.

Clearshift smart film is supplied direct from us to your site, ready to install. The film itself is self-adhesive — applied by your usual glazier or window tinter in a dry install.

The 60V AC controller side is extra-low-voltage and can be terminated by the installer. The 240V mains connection from the controller to wall power must be done by a licensed electrician under AS/NZS 3000.

Order to spec

Send us the dimensions and the wire-exit edge. We cut and pre-attach the bus bars.

We ship direct

Film, controller and connection kit dispatched to your site. 7–14 days door to door.

Bench test

Power up before applying. 30-second sanity check catches any rare faulty panel.

Install & seal

Dry-apply to clean glass, sealed edges with non-acid silicone. Electrician completes the mains connection.
