[ 04 / FAQ ]

Questions you'll get
from your client.

Use these answers. They work.

How long does it last?

Properly installed PDLC film carries a manufacturer life expectation of 7–10 years in normal indoor conditions. Direct UV and moisture ingress at unsealed edges are the two biggest enemies — which is why we ship every film with the edge-sealing kit.

Who wires it up?

The film itself operates at 60V AC (extra-low voltage). The 240V mains connection to the transformer must be done by a licensed electrician under AS/NZS 3000. Our controllers are RCM-marked and ready to install on the load side.

Can I retrofit it to existing windows?

Yes — that's the whole point. The film applies to existing glass (interior side) with its self-adhesive backing. Clean glass, careful application, sealed edges. Allow ~30 minutes per square metre for a careful installer.

What does the end client pay?

Typical residential installs in Australia retail between A$500–1,600 per square metre installed depending on size, scope and trade. Your margin sits in the middle and is yours to set.

What if a sheet arrives faulty?

Bench-test before install — apply to a temporary glass test panel, power up. If the sheet has dead zones, hazing inconsistency or won't switch, photograph and email within 7 days of delivery. We replace.

Is it actually private when frosted?

Yes — visually opaque. You see colour and light through it but no detail or silhouette. Suitable for bathrooms, meeting rooms, medical rooms, executive offices.
